Hi, my name is Benson.

I was raised in Pittsburgh, PA, and I am now based in NYC. I graduated from NYU with a bachelor's in Computer Science and a minor in Child and Adolescent Mental Health.

I like to think about the role of technology in our everyday lives. I am interested in finding solutions for digital addiction and information overload on both personal and systemic levels.

Here are the voices that have influenced my thoughts on the matter:

  • Johann Hari (Stolen Focus)
  • Center for Humane Technology
    • Tristan Harris
    • Aza Raskin
  • Jonathan Haidt
  • Jules Terpak
  • Dr. Alok Kanojia (HealthyGamerGG)
  • Marshall McLuhan
  • Neil Postman

On this site, you'll find my attempt at cultivating a digital garden. You will find meditations, albums, publications, creators, and words of wisdom.
